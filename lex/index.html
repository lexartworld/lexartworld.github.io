Who We Are?

We are LexArtworld, a design and branding agency with partners worldwide. We design thoughtful digital experiences and beautiful brand aesthetics.

Founded in 2018 by Olayinka Abdulazees, Lex Artworld has come a long way from its beginnings in Lagos, Nigeria. When Olayinka first started out, his passion for visual expression drove him to develop an interest in design & marketing so that Lex Artworld can help you bring your imagination to reality. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website. We hope you enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Sincerely, Olayinka Abdulazees

What we do

We are proud of what we do. Our services are intended to help your company stand out by transforming your ideas into digital realities.

Brand Identity

We create brand identities that reflect your beliefs, how you explain your product, and how you want people to feel when they connect with it.

Marketing

We can also help create processes for communicating, delivering and exchanging offerings that have value to your customers, clients, partners, and society at large.

Photography

We also assist with the creation of long-lasting pictures for your company in order to increase sales and internet presence. Good photographs frequently promote a brand before anything else.

UI Design

A visually appealing user interface is critical for keeping your customers happy while they navigate your website or application. A decent user interface will leave a pleasant memory in the minds of your customers.
